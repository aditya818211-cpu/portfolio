Profile Overview

Translating User Needs into Practical Engineering Solutions

I am Aditya Pal, a Mechanical Engineer with hands-on expertise spanning conceptual CAD modeling to physical manufacturing processes.

My engineering philosophy centers around problem-solving, rapid prototyping, and process-oriented design. Equipped with a strong framework in SolidWorks, complex assemblies, and GD&T, I focus on turning real-world requirements into high-fidelity parametric models optimized for manufacturing (DFM/DFA).

Whether researching nano-graphene reinforced composites, observing high-volume HDPE injection molding operations at Poly White Pvt. Ltd., or currently developing the "Stride" assistive device for Parkinson's patients at Ripple Healthcare, I excel at closing the loop between initial user research and final product validation.
